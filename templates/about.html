About MindSarthi AI

Bridging Technology and Professional Care for Mental Wellness

Our Mission

MindSarthi AI is an AI-Powered Mental Health Support & Assistance Platform designed to provide comprehensive mental health services to people of all ages. We bridge the gap between technology and professional care, offering both AI-driven support and access to verified mental health professionals.

What We Offer

Professional Counseling

Connect with verified and trusted mental health professionals for personalized support.

Psychology Courses

Learn essential psychology concepts through structured courses and earn certificates upon completion.

AI Chatbot

An empathetic conversational assistant for immediate support, reflection, and coping strategies.

Privacy & Security

All services prioritize user privacy and data protection with industry-standard encryption.

Why Choose MindSarthi AI?

With psychological cases increasing daily, we provide accessible, affordable, and professional mental health support. Our platform serves all age groups and combines the best of AI technology with human expertise to ensure comprehensive care.

⚠️ In Crisis? If you or anyone is in crisis, contact your local emergency services or crisis hotline immediately.
